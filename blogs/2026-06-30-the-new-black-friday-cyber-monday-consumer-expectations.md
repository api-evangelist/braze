Explores how Black Friday Cyber Monday has evolved from a single sale day into an extended, global shopping season. Braze research found only 9% of consumers said brands exceeded their expectations during the 2025 BFCM season, arguing retailers need individualized, data- and AI-driven engagement rather than blanket discounts.
